- Use clean, professional templates
- Avoid graphics and images
- Use consistent formatting
- Ensure good readability

**3. Vague Project Descriptions**
- Be specific about your contributions
- Quantify achievements when possible

- Customize for each application
- Highlight relevant skills

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best internship for CSE students in India?

The best internship for CSE students in India depends on your skills and career goals. For top-tier experience, Google Summer of Code (₹2.5-5 lakh stipend) and Microsoft Explore (₹50,000/month) are excellent. For beginners, Internshala offers free internships with certificates to build initial experience.

### How can I get a free internship for CSE students with certificate?

You can get free internships with certificates from platforms like Internshala, HackerRank, and GeeksforGeeks. Apply to work-from-home internships, contribute to open source projects on GitHub, or approach college professors for research internships. These provide valuable experience and certificates without requiring financial investment.

### What is the stipend for CSE internships in top companies?

Top companies offer competitive stipends: Google Summer of Code ($3000-6000), Microsoft Explore (₹50,000/month), Amazon (₹80,000/month), Flipkart STEP (₹60,000/month), and Zoho (₹25,000-35,000/month). Mid-tier companies and startups typically offer ₹15,000-30,000 per month.

### When should I apply for summer internships?

Apply for summer internships 3-6 months in advance. For programs like Google Summer of Code, applications open in March for May-July internships. For company-specific programs like Microsoft Explore, applications are in September-October for the following summer. Start preparing your resume and portfolio at least 6 months before.

### Can first-year CSE students get internships?

Yes, first-year CSE students can get internships, though opportunities are limited. Focus on free internships on Internshala, contribute to open source projects, or approach college professors for research internships. Build your skills and portfolio during first year to qualify for better opportunities in second and third year.

### What skills are required for CSE internships?

Required skills vary by role but commonly include: programming languages (Python, Java, JavaScript), web development (React, Node.js), databases (SQL, MongoDB), version control (Git), and problem-solving (DSA). Soft skills like communication and teamwork are equally important. Check specific job descriptions for required technologies.

### How do I prepare for internship interviews?

Prepare for internship interviews by practicing DSA problems on LeetCode and GeeksforGeeks, knowing your projects in depth, preparing for HR questions, and researching the company. Take mock interviews, practice coding under time pressure, and be ready to explain your thought process clearly during technical rounds.

### Are online internships valuable for CSE students?

Yes, online internships are valuable for CSE students. They provide flexibility, global opportunities, and remote work experience. Many top companies offer remote internships. Online internships help build skills, add to your resume, and can lead to full-time remote positions. They're especially good for students who cannot relocate.

### What is the difference between paid and unpaid internships?

Paid internships offer stipend (₹5,000-1,00,000/month) and are more competitive. Unpaid internships focus on learning and certificates, are easier to get, and good for beginners. Paid internships are common at established companies, while unpaid internships are typical at startups, NGOs, and for research positions.

### How many internships should a CSE student complete?

Aim for 2-3 quality internships during your engineering degree. One in second year (free/low stipend for learning), one in third year (paid for experience), and one pre-final year (top company for placement advantage). Quality matters more than quantity—one excellent internship at a top company is better than multiple mediocre ones.
